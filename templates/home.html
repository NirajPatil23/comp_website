The Jawaharlal Nehru Port Authority (JNPA) at Navi Mumbai is a premier container handling Port in India accounting for around 50%
of the total containerised cargo volume, across the major ports of India. Commissioned on 26th May 1989, in less than three decades of its operations,
JNPA has transformed from a bulk cargo terminal to the premier container port in the country. Ranked 26th among the top 100 Container Ports in the world,
JNPA is connected to over 200 ports in the world.
